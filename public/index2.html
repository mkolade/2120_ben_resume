My journey

Early Life and Beginnings

My journey into the world of entrepreneurship began in 2020 on the vibrant streets of Lagos, Nigeria. Born with a vision for financial empowerment, I navigated the early challenges of trading, laying the foundation for my future endeavors. My innate drive and resilience during these formative years shaped my entrepreneurial spirit, setting the stage for the impactful ventures that would follow.

Launch of Itrade

In pursuit of creating meaningful opportunities, I founded Itrade, a platform that transcends traditional boundaries. Itrade, established with the core belief that everyone holds valuable assets, allows users to exchange gift cards for cash at fair rates. The platform quickly gained recognition for its commitment to integrity, fair dealings, and quality service, earning positive reviews from satisfied customers. My dedication to providing a seamless and trustworthy gift card exchange experience has become synonymous with Itrade's success.

Digital Entrepreneurship and Vision for the Future

My entrepreneurial journey extends beyond the streets of Lagos. With a keen eye for trends and a passion for digital innovation, I expanded my horizons into the realm of digital entrepreneurship. Guided by a vision to empower individuals and identify opportunities that unlock their potential, I continue to explore the ever-evolving landscape of digital business. Whether offering trading expertise, personalized investment guidance, or facilitating seamless digital transactions, I envision a future where financial growth is accessible to all.
